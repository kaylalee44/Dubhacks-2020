Meet Roxy

Hi! I'm Roxy, a 12 year old cockapoo that is full of energy. I love playing fetch, eating treats, and long walks on the beach. I am a people dog. If you like to walk in the woods and going for runs, we will get a long perfectly!

Likes

  • Going on long walks
  • Barking and making new friends
  • Playing fetch
  • Winning in tug-a-war
  • Good food (especially treats)
  • Good company

Dislikes

  • Evil squirrels
  • Terrifying fireworks
  • Scary thunderstorms
  • Swimming
