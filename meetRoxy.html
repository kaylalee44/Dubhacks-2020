Meet Roxy

Hi! I'm Roxy, a 12 year old cockapoo who is full of energy. I love playing fetch, eating treats, and long walks on the beach. I am a people dog and I love everyone! If you give me food then you'll have a friend for life.

Likes

  • Going on long walks
  • Barking
  • Playing fetch
  • Making new friends
  • Good food (especially treats)
  • Good company

Dislikes

  • Evil squirrels
  • Terrifying fireworks
  • Scary thunderstorms
  • Swimming
